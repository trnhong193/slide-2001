System Requirements: Camera

  • Camera
  • Quantity: 15 IP cameras (RTSP-capable)
  • Recommended minimum resolution: 1080p at 25 fps
  • Connectivity Type: IP-based cameras with RTSP support
  • Camera placement:
  • Clear view of workers in operational areas for PPE and human-down detection
  • Coverage of vehicle movement corridors for anti-collision
  • Coverage of danger/restricted zones for zone monitoring
  • Coverage of fire/smoke risk areas for fire & smoke detection
